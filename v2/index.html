abstract

MIT's premier competitive robotics team.

The MIT Autonomous Robotics Team is a diverse group of undergraduate students with a common passion for robotics. We aim to explore exciting new technologies, learn critical skills, and promote the field of robotics through entering a variety of competitions and engaging in outreach events in the wider community. We're always looking for dedicated people to work on exciting problems. Join us!
